# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) verbatim. Summary:

- Be respectful and assume good faith.
- No harassment, discrimination, or sexualised content.
- Disagree on ideas, not on people.

Reports: open a private security advisory or email the maintainers. All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this code may face temporary or permanent repercussions.

Full text: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>
